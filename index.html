How sharalike was born

sharalike started after observing a common problem that many photo applications and businesses face: too many photos!

While developing photo applications, we realized the hours spent manually sorting through duplicate and low-quality photos drained our time and energy better spent developing products and serving customers.

We were determined to develop a solution.

Thus, sharalike was born to help developers and product managers create stunning photo apps.

Over 10 years later, sharalike’s reach has extended globally with over 42 million photos turned into videos. We continue to be the photo-sharing solution trusted by leading cloud providers and photo printing providers alike.
